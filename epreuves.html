Life, death and revival of sports over time

Olympic sports are organized by a hierarchy of sports, disciplines and events. A sport or discipline enters the Olympic program if it’s deemed widely practiced (according to the number of countries taking part, with different, more stringents requirements for men VS women and winter VS summer games). Looking at the summer games, we want to see how the number of events per discipline evolves over time, with some remaining quite stable, whereas other more popular sports see considerable evolutions.
